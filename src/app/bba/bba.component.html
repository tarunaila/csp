Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA is an undergraduate degree programme in business administration and management, and the BBA course duration is three years. BBA is considered a career gateway for those candidates who are interested in pursuing a career in business management. BBA full form is Bachelor of Business Administration. BBA is one of the most sought-after undergraduate programmes for students after 10+2. BBA programme serves as a foundation for a wide range of job opportunities in a variety of industries, including administration, education, marketing, sales, finance, and banking.

Degree Name BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
Degree Type Undergraduate
Duration Three years
Eligibility 10+2
Academic Session Semester Based
Admission Process Direct Admission/ Merit Based
Mode of Learning Full Time
Career options after the course Finance Manager, Business Administrator, Human Resource Executive, Research and Development Manager, Business Consultant, Marketing Executive, Sales Executive.

BBA Eligibility Criteria

Candidates can opt for a regular full-time BBA education or distance BBA degree depending upon their area of interest. For most of the colleges that offer online BBA degrees, basic eligibility remains the same. However, some colleges offering BBA courses may instil some changes in BBA eligibility criteria accordingly. Before enrolling for the BBA degree programme, students should know all the BBA course information. Given below are the basic eligibility criteria for the Bachelor of Business Administration or BBA course that a candidate should have in order to pursue a course:

  • Qualifying Exam - Candidates who have completed their 10+2 level in any stream from any recognised educational board are eligible to apply for the degree in bBachelor of Business Administration (BBA).
  • Qualifying Marks - Candidates must have an aggregate score of 50 per cent or above in Class 12

BBA Application Process

BBA admission is offered only on the basis of the scores obtained by candidates in 10+2, entrance examination, be it national level, state level or university level. After qualifing the entrance examination, students need to appear for the GD-PI depending upon selection process.
