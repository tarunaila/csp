B.Sc Information Technology

B.Sc. IT (Information Technology) is a three-year-long undergraduate degree in information technology. Information technology is concerned with the storage, processing, and management of an organization's data. The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology programme is to prepare students how to manage large amounts of information of a company, evaluate the operation of computer systems and network servers, and prevent unnecessary difficulties in data transmission. After completing a programme of study in software development, software design, software engineering, website development, databases, programming, computer networking, and computer systems, a B.Sc. degree in IT is offered.

Degree Name B.Sc in Information Technology
Degree Type Undergraduate
Degree Duration 3 years
Admission process Entrance/Merit
Job profiles Graphic Designer, Software Developer, Computer Hardware Engineer, Network Technician

B.Sc Chemistry

B.Sc. Chemistry is a three years long undergraduate course in the Science stream. The course teaches students about various aspects of chemistry including chemical reactions and compositions of substances, their properties and structure. It consists of subjects like Atomic Structure, Stereochemistry, Bioinorganic Chemistry and others. In order to get admission to a B.Sc. degree course, a student must have a 10+2 with chemistry as one of the subjects from a recognised board. The admission to the UG degree programme is done on both merit and entrance basis.

Degree Name B.Sc in Chemistry
Degree Type Undergraduate
Degree Duration 3 years
Admission process Entrance/Merit
Job profiles Chemist, Lab Technician, Assistant Professor

B.Sc Statistics

BSc Statistics is a 3 year undergraduate course after 12th divided into 6 semesters that broadly deals with the areas of Probability and Statistical Methods, Survey Sampling, Numerical Analysis, etc that is essential for statistical analysis and solving statistical problems. The course includes lectures, flying and simulation training, assignments etc aimed at offering classroom, and practical training as well.

Degree Name B.Sc in Statistics
Degree Type Undergraduate
Degree Duration 3 years
Admission process Entrance/Merit
Job profiles Financial Analyst, Financial Accountant,Operations Officer-Banking
